# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The AI-NIDS project is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people. We pledge to create an environment of mutual respect and psychological safety for everyone who participates in our community, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Caste
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Familial status
- Gender identity and expression
- Marital status
- National origin
- Political beliefs
- Race
- Religion
- Sex
- Sexual orientation
- Socioeconomic status
- Technical experience
- Veteran status

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

‚úÖ **Be Respectful**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

‚úÖ **Be Professional**
- Keep discussions focused and on-topic
- Provide constructive feedback with specific suggestions
- Ask clarifying questions rather than making assumptions
- Give credit and acknowledge others' contributions

‚úÖ **Be Honest**
- Report issues and bugs clearly
- Admit mistakes and learn from them
- Avoid spreading false information
- Be transparent about conflicts of interest

‚úÖ **Be Inclusive**
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Use inclusive language and terminology
- Create opportunities for underrepresented groups
- Amplify marginalized voices

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

‚ùå **Harassment and Discrimination**
- Offensive comments related to protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Stalking or following

‚ùå **Hostile Conduct**
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments
- Attacks on personal or professional identity
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Publishing private communications
- Any form of harassment

‚ùå **Unethical Behavior**
- Spam or self-promotion
- Malicious code contributions
- Attempting to undermine project security
- Using the project for illegal purposes
- Violating others' intellectual property

‚ùå **Bad Faith Contributions**
- Intentionally introducing bugs or vulnerabilities
- Making pull requests solely to create conflict
- Reporting false security issues
- Submitting plagiarized code

## Our Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for:

1. **Clarifying** standards of acceptable behavior
2. **Enforcing** standards fairly and consistently
3. **Taking appropriate action** in response to violations
4. **Protecting privacy** of reporters
5. **Communicating consequences** transparently

We commit to:
- Addressing reports within 5 business days
- Keeping thorough documentation
- Protecting confidentiality of reporters
- Taking privacy and safety seriously
- Reviewing and improving policies regularly

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (repositories, issues, discussions, wikis)
- Public spaces where someone is representing the project
- Private communications related to project business
- Social media posts about the project
- Project-related events (online and in-person)

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

**Report to**: conduct@ai-nids.dev
**Alternative**: DM project maintainers on GitHub

Please include:
- Description of the incident
- When and where it occurred
- Who was involved
- Any relevant links or screenshots
- How it affected you

**Your report will be:**
- Kept confidential (name not shared unless you consent)
- Investigated within 5 business days
- Responded to with clear next steps

### Confidentiality

- The person reporting will not be named without consent
- Only project maintainers will see the report
- Law enforcement will only be contacted if reporting illegal activity

---

## Enforcement Actions

Community leaders may take action including:

| Violation Severity | Potential Actions |
|-------------------|------------------|
| **Minor** (first-time rudeness) | Warning, private discussion |
| **Moderate** (repeated or serious) | Temporary ban from communications |
| **Serious** (harassment, threats) | Immediate ban from community |
| **Critical** (illegal activity) | Law enforcement involvement |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I accidentally violate the Code of Conduct?**  
A: If you made an honest mistake, acknowledge it, apologize, and correct your behavior. We believe in learning and growth.

**Q: What if I feel unfairly treated?**  
A: You can appeal to the core maintainers. Include your side of the story and any additional context.

**Q: Can I be reinstated after being banned?**  
A: Yes, if you demonstrate genuine understanding of the issue and commitment to positive change.

**Q: Are there exceptions to the Code of Conduct?**  
A: No. The Code of Conduct applies equally to all community members, including maintainers.

**Q: How often is this updated?**  
A: At least annually, or when community feedback suggests changes.
